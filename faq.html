  • DONATION

  • 01. How can I donate to your organization?

    We offer multiple ways to donate to our organization. You can make a donation online through our secure donation platform on our website. Additionally, we accept donations via check or money order, which can be mailed to our organization's address. If you prefer to donate in person, we also have designated drop-off locations where you can contribute your donation.

  • 02. Is my donation tax-deductible?

    Yes, your donation is tax-deductible, as we are a registered nonprofit organization. Upon completing your donation, you will receive a receipt that can be used for tax purposes. It is important to consult with a tax professional or refer to your local tax regulations to determine the specific details and requirements for claiming a tax deduction based on your donation.

  • 03. Can I specify how my donation will be used?

    Absolutely! We value transparency and want to honor your intentions. While making a donation, you can indicate any specific area or program you would like your contribution to support. Whether it's a particular project, initiative, or general operating costs, we strive to accommodate donor preferences to the best of our abilities. However, please note that in some cases, restrictions on the use of funds may apply, such as grants with predetermined purposes or legal requirements.

  • ADD RECIPE

  • 01. How do I submit a recipe to your website?

    To submit a recipe, you can navigate to the "Submit Recipe" page on our website. There, you will find a form where you can enter all the necessary details, such as the recipe name, ingredients, measurements, instructions, and any additional notes or tips. You may also have the option to upload accompanying photos of the dish. Once you submit the form, our team will review the recipe for quality and suitability before publishing it on our platform.

  • 02. Can I modify a recipe I submitted after it has been published?

    Yes, in most cases, you can modify a recipe you submitted after it has been published. If you need to make changes, such as correcting a measurement, adding additional instructions, or updating the ingredients, you can typically do so by logging into your account and accessing the "My Recipes" section. From there, you can locate your recipe and make the desired modifications. However, it's important to note that significant changes may require a review by our team before the updated recipe is visible to other users.

  • 03. Can I use recipes from your website for personal or commercial purposes?

    The usage rights for recipes on our website may vary. In general, the recipes provided on our platform are intended for personal use and enjoyment. If you wish to use a recipe for commercial purposes, such as in a restaurant or a food product, it's important to review the specific usage guidelines associated with each recipe. Some recipes may have explicit restrictions or require permission from the recipe creator or copyright holder for commercial utilization. We recommend reaching out to our team or the recipe creator directly to obtain the necessary permissions or licenses for commercial use.

  • MANAGE USER ACCOUNT

  • 01. How do I create an account on your website?

    To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button on our website's homepage. You will be prompted to provide some basic information, such as your name, email address, and a password. Once you submit the registration form, you should receive a confirmation email with instructions to verify your account. Follow the provided link or instructions to complete the account creation process and gain access to your user account.

  • 02. How can I reset my password if I forget it?

    If you forget your password, you can easily reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be asked to enter the email address associated with your account. After submitting the form, you will receive an email with a password reset link. Click on the link and follow the instructions to set a new password for your account. Make sure to choose a strong and unique password to ensure the security of your account.

  • 03. How can I update my account information, such as my email address or profile details?

    To update your account information, log into your user account and navigate to the "Account Settings" or "Profile" section. There, you should find options to edit your personal details, such as your email address, username, profile picture, and any additional information you provided during registration. Make the necessary changes and save your updates. If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team, and they will be happy to assist you.

  • 04. How can I delete my account?

    If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team directly through the designated contact form or email address. Let us know your request to delete your account, and our team will assist you with the process. It's important to note that deleting your account will result in the permanent removal of all associated data, including your personal information, settings, and any content you have created or uploaded. Once the account is deleted, it cannot be restored, so please consider this decision carefully before proceeding.
